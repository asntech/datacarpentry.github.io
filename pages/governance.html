Governance Committee

The Data Carpentry Governance Committee provides community leadership for the lesson program. The Committee is responsible for overseeing the strategy of the Data Carpentry project, and advocating for the program, its mission, and its community. The committee acts as a point of contact for Data Carpentry, representing the lesson program to The Carpentries Governance, the Core Team, and the wider world. Members of the group volunteer to serve two-year terms.

To contact the Data Carpentry Governance Committee, send an email to dc-governors@lists.carpentries.org.
